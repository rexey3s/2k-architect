About Us

2K is an design and construction company engaged in Architecture, Interior Design and Interior Fit-out works. Our core team, including Vietnamese architects who have studied and worked overseas and foreign architects who are fulfilled with experience in the field, guarantees to provide the client professional services with international standards and a well-understanding of Vietnamese culture and tradition.
With the thinking “from follows function with follows the human scale”, we believe that one’s perception of space is very unique. It can be simply comfortable to amazingly exciting. The variety of human’s behavior in different spaces can lead to many different activities. Nobody but the architects, with the ability to balance form and function, can determine those experiences.

We believe that architectural design is not only about drawings, construction skills or styles but also a process of working together between architects and clients to balance the complex of requirements by providing a range of solutions, with a both functional and aesthetic
Therefor, the company’s foundation is building the relationship between us – the architects – and our clients. This relationship will bring the success for the project. Not only we understand the clients’ needs, expectations and visions, but also do we understand the particular function, schedule and budget will affect their decision. To satisfy each project’s unique demands, we appreciate the involvement of our clients in every single stage, from the beginning to the end, which, we believe, ensures the success of the project.
